Goals and Vision

Oregon State University's Post-Bac Women in Computer Science organization seeks to:

  • Create an open forum where barriers that have, or may, impact us can be discussed and overcome as a group
  • Treat each member as an individual, a student, and a future member of the technology industry, free from stereotype.
  • Invite each member to contribute o a discussion regarding heir classes, experiences, and challenges.
  • Provide help and support for one another, knowing that at some poin each of us will need it
  • Communicate and educate each oher about issues that may be pertinent to women, but may be lost in a larger forum
  • Provide educational and career growth opportunies by sharing resources with each other about Women in Technology conferences, organizations, and scholarships.

We Provide:

  • Weekly online meet-ups with group members
  • Two in person meetings per year at the Post-Bac Computer Science Career Showcase
  • A discussion board with positive, technology focused prompts
  • A pool of shared resources and experiences
  • Live video presentations from students, alumni, and organizations that support women in technology
  • Monthly technical mock interview opportunities
  • Group support and possible participation for personal projects

What Else Can We Do?

That's up to you!

If you're a female post-bac computer science major, please come join us! Your experience, input, and suggestions are valued. Each unique opinion and skill set is how we grow and become better.
