    Need Supplemental Help with a Subject?

    Current student worried about understanding a subject?

    • Oregon State Unversity offers tutoring in computer science subjects for their online students! Just because you can't make it into a classroom to ask for help, it's still there.
      • Net tutoring: Click here to access
      • Peer tutoring: Once you get Piazza up and running for the term, make sure you join the peer tutor group to get access to help from students who have been there, done that, and aced it.
    • Tip from the seniors: Create an online study group with Google Hangouts. The women in the group are in the same classes you are, and collaboration makes computer science fun and understandable

    Incoming or Prospective Student Wanting to Brush up on Math Prior to Classes Starting?

    • Khan Academy : A free online program that numerous students have found beneficial for brushing up on their math skills! It will :
      • Give you a short test and then tailor the math to fill in what you don't know
      • Or, allow you to just learn the subjects you know you need help with
      • Click To Get Started

    Wanting to try your hand at some basic programming, or brush up on the fundamentals of a certain language?

    • Codecademy: A free online program that slowly steps you into a language of your choice. Starts at the bare basics, like what a variable is, and works into more complex topics. It does not cover C/C++.
      • Click To Get Started

    Tips 'n' Tricks to Peek At!

    The IDE (integrated development environment) will vary depending on class and instructor suggestion. The following are popular choices from our members, and becoming familiar with one (or more) of them may be beneficial:

    • Vim : OSU will get you up and running with this one. No download needed.
    • Code::Blocks : A free C/C++ IDE
    • Eclipse : An open source C/C++, Java, and PHP IDE
    • Visual Studio : From the TEACH homepage:
      • 1. On the right side, click on Microsoft Dreamspark Login
      • 2. Find Visual Studio for free, admist other amazing products because you're an OSU student
      • 3. Click!
    • Sublime : Open Source and does it all.

    Women in Tech Organizations

    • Women Who Code : Non-profit dedicated to women in technology

    • Girl Develop It : Non-profit dedicated to providing affordable programs for women interested in programming

    • Girls Who Code: : Non-profit dedicated to inspiring and equiping high school girls with the skills and resources to explore tech

    • National Center for Women and Information Technology : Non-profit working to correct the imbalance of gender diversity in the technology industry

    • Anita Borg Institute : Non-profit centered on women in technology and increasing the representation of women in the technology sector
